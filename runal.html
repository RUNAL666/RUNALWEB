Runal Starfinko - The girl is a witch, the predecessor of demons. Basically, she likes to walk around in an empty place, especially during midnight, likes to carry firearms for fun, or go out hunting over someone, likes to cut people's flesh. By her appearance: she’s like a gangster on earth, that people pay attention to her, and apparently they’re not looking for her, even if she didn’t kill anyone.

She manifests with her pentagram called - Star Power. [Pic.2] The Star Power makes Runal the most powerful witch in which not even one person can defeat her, since after acquiring the Star Power she feels much stronger. After this, she can call her attack.

Runal is also the older sister of her younger brother, Jake. They sometimes play together, they are like best friends for eternity, with a rare meeting with her brother, she reveals her sincere joy with a greeting on the hand, or when they meet, they begin to hug each other.

In the world

In the transition to friendship, she also has friends (just ordinary people). With friends, she pretends to be a friendly person, so as not to show herself as a threat to her friends, she spent more time with them: with their walk, with their travels, and with them at the party.

In her reflection, Runal treats humanity as pathetic insects without consciousness, she considers this because she shows that she is with their creation, with their activities, with their points of view on people, as on a true person, with whom she knows what could would happen to them. And with this, Runal underestimates this world.

The history of Runal

Runal was not such a daring person in the past, her personality was completely different. She used to be a kind, mysterious girl, and she also didn’t rate this world as bad at all, and really loved playing piñatas. Her name was actually Lunar - The Small Star. (Interesting fact from the author: Lunar has almost the same type as Jake.)
