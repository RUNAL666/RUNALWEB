Lunar was not always wished well. She understood everything that there are no good things in this world, she only sees everything around with the behavior of people only madness. Lunar was so worried about herself, what was happening in this world and with people, and why they were talking to her like that. Lunar was completely upset by people’s bad words. Since then, Lunar did not want to walk the streets because, in her words: “What if another person will treat me the same way again?” But Lunar once found an idea to cheer up and bring good luck - to try to become a scientist in the sciences in laboratories. Lunar's reaction to her idea she considers it more interesting for her. And now she's starting to study science.
After studying science (With little experience), Lunar decided to try to make some kind of living creature (Her idea), and she herself came up with such products to create her creature: Liquid of water, Liquid of blood, and a dusty white crystal. Lunar, after working with her creation, her result turned out somehow not at all alive, as if it had turned out completely wrong (She thought). But suddenly... this work is not entirely not so wrong... This liquid can move, Lunar looked at him so convincingly, and the liquid opened several eyes all over the body. Lunar was so suddenly scared by this, but this creature was not even going to touch her, he looked at her like a new owner, and he had to leave Lunar’s desk. Lunar now realized that it was not actually her fault for being a scientist. Lunar now getting pleasure in her fantastic idea, and inserts her idea into her science making diary.

*Exactly a few hours of her works have passed...*

Lunar created 2 more such creatures, she did not imagine that they were like pets for her home. Lunar loved her job as a scientist and her ideas more.

*but... it seems to me that something is wrong here...*
