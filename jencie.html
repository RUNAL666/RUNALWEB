―This is a game about a beautiful adult girl called Jencie Mackenzie. In which, from beginning to end, she becomes a demon and a real killer of this world.

History

Jencie was bored sitting at home with nothing to do while her boyfriend(McHecz) was busy with his work. Then Jencie thought about her walk to some places, without her boyfriend. And now Jencie is going on her own adventure!

One day Jencie decided to take her own risk and walk through the beautiful nature of the Forest. And then something unknown happened in this place...
