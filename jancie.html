History

Jancie was bored sitting at home with nothing to do while her boyfriend(McHecz) was busy with his work. Then Jancie thought about her walk to some places, without her boyfriend. And now Jancie is going on her own adventure!

One day Jancie decided to take her own risk and walk through the beautiful nature of the Forest. And then something unknown happened in this place...
